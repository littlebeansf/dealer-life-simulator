EVERYTHING A DEALER NEEDS

🧬

10 Playable Races

Vampire, Elf, Orc, Goblin, Dragonkin and more. Each race carries unique bonuses, passives, and story paths.

🏙

10 Locations

Travel between dangerous territories. Each district has its own black markets, NPCs, gang presence, and law enforcement.

⚔️

Gang System

6 rival gangs compete for dominance. Form alliances, declare war, extort rivals, and expand your territory block by block.

👥

People & Relations

Build trust with 50+ NPCs. Romance a fence, befriend a guard captain, betray a supplier, or extort a merchant.

📈

Deep Economy

Dynamic prices shift with supply and demand. Manage your heat level, underworld reputation, and public standing carefully.

🎲

Life Events

Random events trigger every year. Age, prosper, fall in love, get arrested, or die. No two runs are ever the same.

HOW TO PLAY

01
🧑

Create Your Character

Pick your race, name, and backstory. Start your life from age 0 with nothing but ambition and a few coins.

02
💊

Trade & Survive

Buy and sell contraband across districts. Manage heat, stamina, and coin. Keep the guards off your back.

03
👑

Build Your Empire

Recruit gang members, expand territory, and climb the underworld hierarchy. Become the most feared dealer alive.
